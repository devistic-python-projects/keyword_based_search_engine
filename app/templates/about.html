    Our Mission

    DocQuery is a powerful keyword-based search engine for text documents designed to help users efficiently search, upload, and manage text documents. Whether you're a student, researcher, or professional, our platform simplifies document discovery.

    Technology

    Built with Flask, SQLite, and Whoosh, DocQuery ensures fast, reliable, and secure document processing. Features like spell-checking, document previews, and intuitive search make it a versatile tool for all users.
