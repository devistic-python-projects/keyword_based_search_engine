About the Search Engine

This Keyword-Based Search Engine allows users to efficiently search and manage text documents. Upload documents, search with keywords or phrases, and benefit from features like spell-checking and document previews.

Built with Flask, SQLite, and Whoosh, it prioritizes speed, accessibility, and ease of use.
