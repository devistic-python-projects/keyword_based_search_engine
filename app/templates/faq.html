Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats are supported?

We support .txt, .csv, .json, .xml, and .tsv files.

Do I need an account to search?

No, you can search and download documents without an account. However, uploading requires an account.

How does spell-checking work?

As you type, misspelled words are underlined with a red wavy line. Right-click for suggestions or hover for corrections.
